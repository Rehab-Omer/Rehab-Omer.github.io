ENJOY FREE SERVICES

    Continuous follow-up:

    With your subscription, your exercise
    performance will be monitored weekly
    to ensure your progress and commitment
    to exercise, and to modify the plan assigned
    to you if necessary.

    Slimming services:

    For those who are tired of being overweight
    and unable to commit to individual exercises,
    the trainer provides you with thoughtful training
    services and plans in addition to a diet that suits
    your needs and to achieve a lower weight in the
    way you desire.

    Services for bodybuilders:

    Dealing with athletes who want to become stars
    in bodybuilding and achieve desired results based
    on scientific methods and thoughtful plans.Of course,
    the trainee will be provided with all the nutritional
    supplements his body needs to be built correctly.

    MOTIVATION AND ACCOUNTABILITY:

    WE can act as a source of motivation as they
    inspire clients to push past their limits
    and stay committed to their fitness goals.
    Your trainer understands that staying motivated
    can be challenging, especially if your fitness
    journey involves specific obstacles or potential setbacks.

    SUPPORT AND COMMUNICATION:

    Knowing how to overcome these obstacles or finding answers
    to questions isn't something a beginner is expected
    to master right away. This is where a teacher comes into play.

    KNOW AND MODIFY ERRORS DURING EXERCISE:

    Detect errors during exercise, correct the
    trainee's sports positions, know
    what the trainee needs to reach his goal safely,
    and reduce the occurrence of any injuries
    that would lead to moving away from exercises.
